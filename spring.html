Tournament Format Highlights

Group Stage (35 Overs)

  • Round Robin format ensures each team plays 4 matches
  • Two regional groups of 5 teams each
  • Matches played over 35 overs per side
  • League table determines qualification for knockouts
  • Provides balanced competition before knockout stages

Plate Competition Benefits

  • Additional competitive matches for teams outside Championship places
  • Shorter, spectator-friendly T20 games for knockout stages
  • Efficient shared venue usage with multiple matches at same location
  • Continued engagement for all teams throughout tournament
  • Knockout excitement with clear path to Plate trophy
